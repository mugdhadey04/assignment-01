Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Innovation in Bangladesh 2.0 is a movement to create a more prosperous and inclusive country through digitalization, entrepreneurship, and sustainable infrastructure

Sustainability

Sustainability is a key component of Bangladesh 2.0, which aims to achieve economic growth while protecting the environment

Infrastructure

Bangladesh 2.0 is a vision for the country to develop its infrastructure and economy through digital innovation, social reform, and sustainable developmen

Empowerment

Empowerment in Bangladesh 2.0 includes efforts to improve the lives of women, youth, and other groups through social reforms, inclusive growth, and sustainable infrastructure.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nations trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.
